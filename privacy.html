Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Overview

RunInterval Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Do Not Collect

RunInterval Pro does not collect any personal data. We do not:

  • Collect your name, email, or any contact information
  • Track your location
  • Access your health data
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Display advertisements
  • Connect to external servers

Local Data Storage

All data created in RunInterval Pro (workouts, intervals, settings) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

App Permissions

The app may request access to:

  • Health app - Only if you choose to save workouts to Apple Health (optional)
  • Notifications - For workout timers (optional)

These permissions are entirely optional and controlled by you through iOS Settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store.
